■ オセロゲームについて
■ About the game of Othello
1．先手、後手の順で1手ずつ打っていきます。
1. Players take turns placing one piece at a time, starting with the first player.
2．縦、横、斜で必ず相手の駒をはさまなければなりません。はさめない時はパスします。
2. You must place your piece so that it outflanks at least one of your opponent’s pieces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally. If no move is possible, you must pass.
3．相手の駒をはさむと、はさまれた駒は自分の駒になります。
3. When you outflank your opponent’s pieces, the captured pieces become your own.
4．勝負は盤上の駒数が多い方が勝ちとなります。
4. The player with more pieces on the board at the end wins.
